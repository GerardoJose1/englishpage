Our product

Our product consist in a recycler robot, the idea arises when as a company we see the pollution caused by the excess of garbage that people generate when throwing it in the street, so as a company we decided to create this robot that will be in charge of collecting the garbageand recycling it, generating a healthier and cleaner environment for our community.

Who is our product for?

Our product is for all people who want to recycle their garbage and thus contribute to the reduction of pollution
